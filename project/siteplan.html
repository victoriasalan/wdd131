Website Planning Document

1. Site Name

Sublipronto

I chose that name since it was the name of a business my dad used to have. It combines the words sublimation and pronto to denote the type of business and the speed and quality of it.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of the Sublipronto website is to promote the business, display available products, and make it easier for customers to request custom items. The site will include:

  • A home page explaining the concept and mission.
  • A product page with descriptions and sample images.
  • An order/contact page with a simple form or table for client requests.

3. Scenarios

  • What types of personalized products does Sublipronto offer?
  • How can I place a custom order or request a quote?

4. Color Schema

Main colors used on the website and where they are going to be used:

#0c6399
Primary color for headers and navigation.

#ffb703
Accent color for buttons or highlights.

#f4f4f4
Background color for light contrast.

#ffffff
Used for main content sections.

5. Typography

The website will use two Google Fonts:

  • Poppins - for headings.
  • Open Sans - for paragraphs and body text.
